Property and Eviction Law

Whether you are a landlord dealing with a non-paying tenant or a tenant facing an unlawful eviction, Nzuza Attorneys provides expert legal advice and representation. We ensure that all eviction processes are handled in strict accordance with the Prevention of Illegal Eviction from and Unlawful Occupation of Land Act (PIE Act).

Our eviction and property services include:

  • Residential and commercial evictions
  • Drafting and reviewing lease agreements
  • Handling rental disputes
  • Representation at the Rental Housing Tribunal
  • Advising on tenant rights and landlord obligations
  • Unlawful occupation matters

Protecting Your Property Rights

We understand that property disputes can be stressful and financially draining. Our approach is to provide swift, effective legal action to protect your interests and resolve disputes as efficiently as possible.
