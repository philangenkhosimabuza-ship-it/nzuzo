Protecting Your Rights

Facing criminal charges can be one of the most stressful experiences of your life. At Nzuza Attorneys, we provide expert criminal defense representation to individuals in Durban and surrounding areas. We are committed to ensuring that your rights are protected and that you receive a fair trial.

Our criminal law expertise includes:

  • Bail applications (after hours and formal)
  • Defense in criminal trials
  • Representation in plea and sentence agreements
  • Expungement of criminal records
  • Appeals and reviews

Why Choose Us for Criminal Defense?

We understand the urgency of criminal matters, which is why we offer 24/7 support for bail applications. Our strategic approach and dedication to our clients have resulted in numerous successful outcomes.
